1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Sky Fury accesses, collects, uses, and shares user data. It applies to the Android app package com.mavelastudio.skyfury.

2. Data We Access, Collect, and Use

Depending on your device, region, and feature usage, the app and integrated services may process:

  • Device and app identifiers (for example, advertising identifiers and app instance identifiers used by SDKs).
  • App activity and diagnostics (for example, crash/performance telemetry from SDK infrastructure).
  • Purchase transaction metadata required to complete and validate in-app purchases (for example, product ID and purchase token).
  • Local gameplay data stored on your device (for example, score, settings, unlocked items, and in-game currency balance).

The app does not require account sign-up and does not request sensitive runtime permissions such as contacts, camera, microphone, or location.

3. Why We Use Data

  • To provide core gameplay and save local progress/settings.
  • To serve ads and prevent fraud/abuse in ad delivery.
  • To process and fulfill in-app purchases.
  • To maintain app stability, reliability, and security.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share data with service providers only as needed for the purposes above. Current providers include:

  • Google Play Services / Google Play Billing (purchase processing and entitlement flow).
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) (ad delivery and related measurement/fraud prevention).

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Gameplay settings/progress are stored locally on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • Purchase records are retained as needed for transaction processing, fraud prevention, legal obligations, and accounting.
  • Third-party providers may retain data according to their own retention schedules and legal obligations.

7. Your Choices and Controls

  • You can stop local data storage by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage from Android settings.
  • You can manage ads personalization using your Google account and Android ad settings.
